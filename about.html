Our Story

2022
The Idea
Three students frustrated with clunky to-do apps decided to build something better over a weekend hackathon.
2023
First Release
TaskFlow v1.0 launched with basic task management, a calendar, and a Pomodoro timer. 500 users in the first week.
2024
Major Redesign
Complete UI overhaul with dark mode, priority tags, and advanced filtering. Grew to 3,000+ active users.
2025
Today & Beyond
We're building team collaboration, AI-powered scheduling, and cross-device sync. The best is yet to come.
